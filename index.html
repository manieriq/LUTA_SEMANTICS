Origin and history of dogs

German Shepherd

Paleontologists and archaeologists have determined that about 60 million years ago a small mammal, rather
like a weasel, lived in the environs of what are now parts of Asia. It is called Miacis,
the genus that became the ancestor of the animals known today as canids: dogs, jackals, wolves, and foxes.
Miacis did not leave direct descendants, but doglike canids evolved from it.
